Daily Wellness Companion is a simple, user-friendly website designed to support individuals in maintaining their mental and physical well-being. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it offers interactive features such as mood tracking, water intake logging, sleep monitoring, motivational quotes, journaling, and personalized recommendations. Whether you're a student, worker, or someone at home, the site adapts to your daily input to suggest whether you should focus on productivity or take time to rest
